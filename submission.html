We invite three kinds of submissions:

  • The page limit for full papers is 12 pages (including references and supplemental material), and must conform to standard ECML/PKDD conference submission guidelines.
  • Short papers, describing ongoing work and vision statements relevant to theworkshop (max 4 pages)
  • Demonstration papers, describing working solutions relevant to the workshop(max 4 pages)

    • Workshop Proceedings:

    The workshop proceedings will be published by Springer in the Lecture Notes in Computer Science series (LNCS). The proceedings will be published after the conference and will include only the papers that authors have presented at the conference. During the conference a version of the papers will be available for conference participants.

    Awards and Journal Publication:

    We will set one best paper award according to the review results and presentation of a paper and best papers will be invited for an extended version in top tier journal venues.

    Submission Process:

    Electronic submissions will be handled via Easy Chair.
